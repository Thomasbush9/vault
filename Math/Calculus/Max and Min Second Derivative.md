The first derivative is 0 when we have a max or a min => slope == 0 

The second derivative tells us whether it's a max or a min. 
The second derivative of the of the distance (speed=df/dt) = d^2 f/ d t^2

We can observed the bending curve of our function. We have the maximum when we have the slope (first derivative) equal to 0. When the second derivative changes sign it means that the bending of our function changes (==inflection point is a point where the second derivative is 0==)
