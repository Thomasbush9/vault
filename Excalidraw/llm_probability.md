Motivations
1. LLMs are integrated into stochastic pipelines -> need to sample from
probability distributions

https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.05414

Proposal: 
1. Train a transformer model (model_p) directly on probability distributions -> performances? 
2. Internal representation: does the mechanism of model_p differ from SOTA LLMs?
